Carioca Instructions — Version 2

A quick, practical guide for the Play vs Computer mode in this app.

Quick start

  1. Draw 1 card (from deck or discard pile).
  2. Lay your objective meld(s) for the round if possible.
  3. If your objective is complete, add cards to existing table melds.
  4. Discard 1 card to end your turn.

You can only draw once per turn, and you must draw before discarding.

Setup

  • 2 players: you and one AI opponent.
  • 2 full decks + 4 jokers (108 cards total).
  • Each player is dealt 12 cards every round.
  • 1 face-up card starts the discard pile.

Card points

  • Joker = 30
  • Ace = 20
  • J/Q/K = 10
  • 2–10 = face value

Meld rules

Set of 3

Three cards of the same rank. At most one joker.

Straight of 4

Four consecutive ranks. Aces can wrap in sequences.

Jokers

Can substitute missing cards. In 13-card straights, jokers cannot be adjacent.

To go out (play your final card), your current round objective must already be complete.

Round objectives (must be completed in order)

Round Objective
1Two three-of-a-kind
2One straight + one three-of-a-kind
3Two straights
4Three three-of-a-kind
5Two three-of-a-kind + one straight
6Two straights + one three-of-a-kind
7Four three-of-a-kind
8Three straights
9Crazy straight (13 cards, any suits)
10Colour straight (13 cards, all red or all black)
11Royal straight (13 cards, same suit)

How scoring and winning work

  • A round ends immediately when a player has no cards left (this can happen after melding, with or without a discard).
  • The other player adds penalty points for all cards left in hand.
  • After round 11, the player with the lowest total score wins.
